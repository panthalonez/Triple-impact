Why are we doing this?

because every discarded morsel is a missed opportunity, a waste of resources, and a stain on our collective conscience. Our "4-Recipe Food Waste Project" with Triple Impact isn't just about cooking; it's about transforming negligence into deliciousness, proving that yesterday's forgotten ingredients—like that leftover rice, slightly wilted vegetables, or even overripe mangoes—can be resurrected into culinary triumphs like flavorful fried rice, hearty dried rice dishes, versatile dried vegetables, and vibrant mango pickle. We're not just making meals; we're sparking a revolution, one delicious, waste-free dish at a time.

TRIPLE IMPACT

To build a sustainable future, schools and communities must raise awareness and change habits to reduce waste. Responsible consumption can decrease waste. Students, families, schools, and shops can share tips on portion control, food sharing, and creative reuse.
