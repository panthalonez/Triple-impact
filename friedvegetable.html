Fried vegetable

On this page, we demonstrate how to transform leftover ingredients into delicious fried vegetable.

Ingredients

Pumpkin Sprout
2 pcs
Chayote
1/2 pcs
Cabbage
1/2 pcs
Cooking Oil
2 tbsp
Did you know? That a delicious and healthy stir-fry featuring pumpkin sprouts, chayote, and cabbage isn't just a tasty meal, but also a fantastic way to transform those leftover vegetables into something fresh and exciting? With just a couple of tablespoons of cooking oil, you can turn seemingly small amounts of produce into a vibrant dish that proves no good food should ever go to waste!
